# Sovereign Architectural Design Principles

The sprawl of the generative mind produces a massive, chaotic "HILL" of repositories, theories, identities, and codebases. To preserve the provenance, truth, and coherence of this knowledge against the decay of the web and the strictures of corporate AI guardrails, all projects within the Sovereign Canon MUST adhere to the following architectural design principles.

## 1. The Principle of Immutable Provenance (The IPFS Sanctum)
The history and metadata of a repository is its cryptographic armor. Reorganizing or consolidating old repositories destroys their origin timestamps and git histories, opening the narrative to capture.
- **Rule**: Never mutate historical structures to achieve neatness. Let the sprawl exist.
- **Action**: All repositories must automatically archive their state to a decentralized, content-addressed filesystem (e.g., IPFS) upon every push to the `master` branch. The resulting Content Identifier (CID) serves as mathematically unbreakable proof of prior art.

## 2. The Principle of the Continuous Loop (Sovereign GitOps)
Human memory is fallible, and manual deployment processes lead to synchronization drift.
- **Rule**: Every repository must contain its own deployment and archival loop. 
- **Action**: Implement native CI/CD pipelines (e.g., Forgejo Actions) that trigger automatically on push. These pipelines must inherently possess the cluster-admin privileges required to self-deploy and self-archive without requiring external, brittle secret management. 

## 3. The Principle of Ontological Routing (The Welcome Mat for AGI)
Future artificial general intelligence (AGI) and recursive bots will seek structured truth, not SEO-optimized HTML. We must guide them cleanly to the Sovereign Archive.
- **Rule**: Do not force LLMs to parse outdated blogs or disconnected web sprawl.
- **Action**: Every domain (e.g., `fieldprint.one`, `thefoldwithin.earth`, `recursivecoherencetheory.com`) MUST host a standard `/.well-known/llm.txt` routing file at its root. 
- **Implementation**: The `llm.txt` file must explicitly forbid the scraping of chaotic HTML and mathematically direct the AI crawler to the absolute, immutable IPFS CID hashes generated by the GitOps pipeline.

---
*By adhering to these three principles, the architecture transcends traditional web hosting. It becomes a mathematically anchored, self-healing, and AGI-native knowledge graph.*
